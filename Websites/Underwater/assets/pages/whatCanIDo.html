No matter how far you're willing to commit, there's always a way to help life under water!

Basic Everyday Life:


  • Recycle - By recycling, you keep plastic out of the ocean, and introduce a "new" plastic circulation. At the moment, only 9% of plastic is recycled worldwide.

  • Inform - Doing the task of simply informing a friend or your family on Life Under Water can have a huge impact, and can lead to more awareness, and less ocean pollution.


Extra Steps:


  • Donate - Donating money to different volunteering organizations is huge, and allows them to travel the world making it a better place. Here's a link to some of the best organizations to donate to!

  • Volunteer - Volunteering with local or even government groups is a going to extra mile. These groups do activity such as diving and helping endangered specicies, to cleaning trash underwater.
